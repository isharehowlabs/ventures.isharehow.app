## üé® Design Principles Applied

### Color Psychology
- **White Background**: Trust, cleanliness, professionalism
- **Indigo/Purple Accents**: Innovation, luxury, expertise
- **Green Checkmarks**: Success, progress, safety
- **Gray Sections**: Visual breaks, content separation

### Typography Hierarchy
- **H1**: 5xl-6xl, bold, gradient text for key phrases
- **H2**: 4xl-5xl, bold, section headers
- **Body**: xl, gray-600 for readability
- **CTAs**: Bold, white text on gradient backgrounds

### Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace (py-20 sections)
- Consistent padding (px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8)
- Grid layouts for scannable content
- Max-width containers (7xl) for readability

### Conversion Optimization
- **Above-the-fold CTA**: Immediate action opportunity
- **Sticky navigation**: Always accessible conversion path
- **Multiple CTA types**: Primary, secondary, tertiary
- **Social proof early**: Stats immediately after hero
- **Benefit-focused copy**: "Transform", "Growth", "Revenue"
