Empowering Lives One Skill at a Time

In many neighborhoods across the country, good-paying, stable jobs feel out of reach for far too many people. Barriers like lack of training, transportation, past records, or simply not knowing where to start keep talented individuals stuck on the sidelines.

Thats where PACT Venture steps in.

We believe that everyone deserves a fair shot at meaningful workand that the property care industry is the perfect place to make it happen. From lawn care and landscaping to cleaning, painting, and general maintenance, these are essential services that will never go away. Better yet, theyre skills anyone can learn quickly and turn into a real career.

Who We Are

PACT Venture is a social-impact organization on a mission to bridge the opportunity gap in disadvantaged communities. We provide hands-on training and guaranteed job placement in the property care sector, helping people build sustainable incomes while strengthening the places we all call home.

Our programs are built around real-world skills that employers need right now:

  • Property Maintenance Repairs, painting, drywall, plumbing basics, and general upkeep
  • Lawn Care & Landscaping Mowing, edging, planting, seasonal clean-ups, and creating beautiful outdoor spaces
  • Professional Cleaning Services Deep cleaning, move-in/move-out, commercial janitorial, and post-construction cleanup
  • And more Were always expanding based on what the market demands

After training, we dont just hand you a certificate and wish you luck. We connect you directly with employers who are actively hiringproperty management companies, landscaping firms, cleaning contractors, and homeowners who need reliable help.

Why Property Care?

Its simple:

  • High demand, low barriers to entry
  • Jobs available in every city and town
  • Flexible schedules (perfect for parents or side-hustlers)
  • Clear paths to entrepreneurshipmany of our graduates start their own crews within a year

When people have steady work, families eat better, kids see positive role models, and entire neighborhoods start to thrive. Thats the ripple effect were creatingone job at a time.
